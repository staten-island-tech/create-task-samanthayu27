Restaurant Recall

How To Play: You're the new chef for a very fancy restaurant. You will recieve a order with an item from the menu, the item's ingredients, and how to prepare the item. Your job is to memorize the order and click one of the options. If you make a mistake in the order, you must restart from the beginning. The difficulty will increase as you progress. Good luck! Don't disapoint the customers or you will get fired!!!
